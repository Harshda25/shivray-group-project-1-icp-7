Who was Shivaji?

The very name of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj sent shivers down the spine of Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb and his generals. The ruler of the Maratha Confederacy, Shivaji was one of the greatest kings of India, whose guerrilla warfare tactics changed the course of mountain warfare strategies. Outwitting the Mughals in numerous ways, Shivaji proved himself to be one of the greatest sons of the soil of India. Read about his exploits in this book, specially illustrated in the style of miniature paintings.

Jijau - The Mother of All Gurus

Jijabai, also known as Rajmata Jijabai, was the mother of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire in India. She played a significant role in Shivaji's upbringing and his early education, instilling in him a sense of pride, valor, and a deep understanding of Hindu traditions and values.
